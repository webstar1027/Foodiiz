About us

Foodiiz is… The favorite website of gourmet travelers! Worldwide leader of social dining, our community is full of thousands of hosts ready to welcome you and show you the real taste of their city. Around the world, or just around the corner, re-discover the surroundings in a delicious and authentic atmosphere.

No need to be a professional chef to start hosting, the only required ingredients are a good sense of hospitality and a pinch of curiosity. From cooking classes, to food tours and dinners, Foodiiz is for everyone that believes that people who love cooking and eating are the best type of people!

With Foodiiz, you’ll never travel the same way again!

Foodiiz is currently available in more than 130 countries. If you can’t find your destination, it means that we are still on the lookout for amazing host in the area. We hope we’ll have an amazing Foodiiz experience available there soon!

Account settings

If you forgot your password or are having trouble logging into your account, access the following page:

https://www.foodiiz.com/accounts/forgotten-password

Type in your email address and we’ll send you an email with a link to reset your password.

If you don't receive an email, check your Spam folder. If you need any help, you can always email us at support@foodiiz.com

To change your current password:

  1. Log in to your account and click your name at the top right corner of the page
  2. Select Account
  3. Select Security on the left
  4. Enter the password you want to use and confirm your password by typing it a second time

Click Update to save the changes

To change your email address:

  1. Log in to your account and click your name at the top right corner
  2. Select Account
  3. Select Security on the left
  4. Next to Email, enter the email address you want to use

Click Update to save the changes

Your email address can only be associated with one Foodiiz account at a time. If you get a notification that your email address is already in use but you can't remember your password for that account, you can reset your password.
